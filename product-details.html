Lots of Information for
Technical Decisions

Mechanical Seaming

Unlike traditional roofing that uses screws, our standing seam panels are mechanically folded over each other using specialized robots. This creates a 100% puncture-free roof surface, fundamentally eliminating the possibility of leaks.

Thermal Expansion

The panels are secured using a concealed clip system that allows the steel to slide during heat-induced expansion and contraction. This prevents oil-canning (rippling) and ensures the roof remains flat and beautiful in extreme climates.

Longevity & ROI

With an expected lifespan exceeding 50 years, the Standing Seam system offers the lowest total cost of ownership in the industry. It requires zero maintenance and significantly increases the resale value of any property.

Acoustic Dampening

Optional integrated acoustic layers can be installed between the panel and the substrate, effectively reducing noise from heavy rainfall or hail by up to 25 decibels, creating a peaceful indoor environment.

Alu-Zinc Technology

Our steel is coated with a precise alloy of Aluminum, Zinc, and Silicon. The aluminum provides barrier protection against corrosion, while the zinc offers sacrificial protection for any cut edges or scratches.

Solar Integration

The standing seam system is the perfect substrate for solar PV panels. Specialized S-5! clips allow for solar mounting without a single roof penetration, maintaining the warranty while generating clean energy.
